Prague, a holiday you’ll never forget

© EmmaKarolina

Original drawing, ink and graphite on paper, 24 × 30 cm, 2023

A scene from Prague, depicting broken love — the pain of giving love to someone who cannot receive it, and the confusion of gaslighting.

More Info ▼
This artwork captures the emotional struggle of a one-sided relationship, where love is offered but not truly accepted. It evokes feelings of heartbreak, manipulation, and entrapment, set against the backdrop of a historic city. The tension between vulnerability and control is central to the scene.
Full Story ▼
In this drawing, a large profile of a person holding an anatomical heart is placed to the left. Nearby, a figure resembling Humpty Dumpty, chained and unable to break free from his past, symbolizes distortion of truth and manipulation. Various objects scatter the scene, including a hat, a champagne bottle, and fallen chess pieces, hinting at memories and lost control. A large, menacing hippopotamus appears, symbolizing an underlying threat. Chained animals and a vulture flying near a building reinforce the theme of captivity and emotional imprisonment. A violin references a concert attended during the journey, and subtle images of the Pokémon Froakie hint at a fleeting moment of joy amid tension. Above, a plane descends toward crash landing, reflecting feelings of destruction and despair. Mushrooms growing on a building emphasize decay and change. The famous Prague tower, with a tunnel-like street beneath, anchors the composition, merging the personal narrative with the cityscape.
