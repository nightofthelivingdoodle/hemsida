I See You See Me

A figure sits quietly, an eye where a face should be — inviting a gentle reflection on what it means to truly see and be seen. Surrounded by flowing branches and resting on a curled tail, the image speaks to the delicate balance between inner awareness and outward presence. It’s less about specifics and more about feeling your own gaze, acknowledging your needs, and embracing the quiet strength found in self-recognition.
